Capsule machine Quechua


One of our all favorite products the Quechua pod machine is ideal for every coffe lover who prefers a simple handling and a quick pouring! Compatible with our own and all Nespresso compatible Capsules. Machine heats up in only 15 seconds and comes with a removable 1L water tank. The used capsule container can hold up to 20 used capsules. Cup support can be adjusted for tall glasses. off mode after 10min of inactivity.
