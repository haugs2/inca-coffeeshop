About us

We are a local company from Burgdorf who specialises in distributing fair trade coffee from Latin American countries. We work with fair trade coffee producers in Peru, Venezuela and Colombia. Our focus lies on fair trade high quality coffee. We also sell coffee cups as well has premium coffee machines. Our cups are produced in Peru by indigenous manufacturers. Our coffee machines are produced in Switzerland and Latin America. Do not hesitate to contact us for more information about our products!

Our passion is keeping the love for premium coffee alive across the world!
